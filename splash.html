• About
  • StraVS. is a competitive biking and running application built using the Strava API. Users sign in with an exisiting Strava account and form teams with their friends or peers. Teams challenge eachother to week-long battles of endurance and athletics. A winner is determined at the end of the week by our super sick algorithm which is currently in creation.
